About Me

I'm a dedicated and passionate Information Technology student at Ateneo de Davao University, currently in my second year of pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how software shapes our daily lives, and it has evolved into a deep commitment to becoming a skilled software engineer.

Throughout my academic journey, I've maintained excellence in my studies, consistently earning spots on the President's List and Dean's List. This academic success reflects my dedication to learning and my ability to balance multiple responsibilities while maintaining high standards.

Beyond academics, I actively engage in various organizations and volunteer work. As the Merchandise Head for CSSEC Wizards, I've honed my leadership and organizational skills while contributing to major events like U-Fest 2024 and IT Week 2025. My involvement in BAHAGHARI-AdDU and the Ateneo Blue Knight Parliament demonstrates my commitment to inclusivity, diversity, and student governance.

My technical interests span web development, desktop applications, and UI/UX design. I enjoy creating solutions that not only function well but also provide excellent user experiences. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to community service projects, or working on research that bridges technology and social impact.
